ABOUT SCHOOL

This school is an important part of the World University of Design. It offers courses in Business & Management. It has an alignment to creativity, Design thinking, Innovation & Future thinking. Any field under the sun needs an impetus to survive with a good business structure. The School of Business provides the same through its studies. It has courses with a collaboration of Design subjects and Business Management so that the students are enabled with a holistic capacity to be adept to the future. To be able to generate business in all the fields possible. It also has a strong foundation of Design thinking & Design strategy that enables Innovation capacity building within its learning environment. The School of Business envisions creating empathetic leaders for the future with a positive impact.

World University of Design

The World University of Design (WUD), established in 2018, is a private institution located in Sonipat, Haryana, India. It holds the distinction of being India's first and only university dedicated exclusively to education in the creative domains.
